How to Use CosmoZoom

Your journey from beginner to expert space explorer

1

Launch the Explorer

Click "Launch Explorer" to access our advanced image analysis platform with real NASA data from JWST and Hubble telescopes.

2

Select or Upload Images

Choose from our curated collection of stunning space images or upload your own astronomical data for analysis.

3

Zoom and Explore

Use mouse wheel or zoom controls to explore images at pixel level. Track coordinates and discover hidden details.

4

Run AI Analysis

Let our advanced AI automatically detect celestial objects, patterns, and anomalies with detailed metadata.

Make Discoveries

Annotate findings, save research notes, and contribute to citizen science by identifying new phenomena.

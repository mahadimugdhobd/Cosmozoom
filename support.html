Quick Help

How do I zoom into an image?

Use your mouse wheel to zoom in and out, or click the zoom buttons in the toolbar. You can also use the + and - keys on your keyboard.

What image formats are supported?

CosmoZoom supports JPG, PNG, and FITS formats. For best results, use high-resolution images from NASA archives.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI pattern recognition achieves 97%+ accuracy for common celestial objects. Results are continuously improved through community feedback.

Is CosmoZoom available on mobile?

Yes! CosmoZoom works on tablets and mobile devices. Native iOS and Android apps are coming in 2025.

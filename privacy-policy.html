Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

CosmoZoom is committed to protecting your privacy while enabling groundbreaking space exploration. We collect:

  • Usage Data: Image viewing patterns, zoom levels, and annotation activities
  • Contributions: Labels, annotations, and discoveries you make
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and performance metrics
  • Account Information: If you create an account, basic profile information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us improve space exploration and scientific discovery:

  • Enhance AI pattern recognition algorithms
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Enable collaborative citizen science projects
  • Generate insights for NASA and the scientific community

3. Data Sharing and NASA Collaboration

As part of the NASA Space Apps Challenge, we may share anonymized data with:

  • NASA and affiliated research institutions
  • Academic researchers studying space phenomena
  • The global citizen science community

All shared data is anonymized and used solely for scientific advancement.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure cloud infrastructure

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Export your contributions

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact Team Mugdho:

  • Email: privacy@cosmozoom.space
  • GitHub: github.com/team-mugdho/cosmozoom
  • NASA Space Apps: spaceapps.nasa.gov
